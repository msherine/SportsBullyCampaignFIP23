Bullying In Swimming

EMERGE

“ We raise awareness and spark conversation regarding the sport of “Swimming” and the bullying being faced by the people in the sport. “

Behind ‘Emerge’

By raising awareness about bullying in swimming, the chance of bullying can be reduced, swimmers can be helped to perform better on the field, and people can be empowered to truly enjoy swimming.

Our campaign “Emerge” is based on the sport Swimming and bullying existing in the sport. Going by the literal definition, “Emerge” means to begin to exist and to come to an end of a difficult period or experience. And by this campaign we want people in swimming to feel safe.

Discussing about bullying in swimming, there are lots of ways people are bullied. ​First by the audience, swimming being a lesser known sport & neglecting the efforts of swimmers is a type of bullying. ​There are other cases of bullying as well, some being so severe that free psychotherapy sessions were provided to cope up with the trauma. ​To bring this seriousness out to the world, the campaign Emerge was initiated.
